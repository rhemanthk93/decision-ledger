Daniel: Right. So the deep cause is that our deploy policy is implicit. Each PR author decides for themselves whether their change is "safe enough" to auto-deploy, and for most things it is. If we want the convenience of auto-deploy for low-risk changes, we can add an allow-list later. The default should be staging first.
